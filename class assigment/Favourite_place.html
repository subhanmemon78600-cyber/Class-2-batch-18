MY FAVOURITE PLACE:KASHMIR

Azad Jammu & Kashmir ek aisa sheher nahi, balki ek jadoo hai jo apne pahaadon, nadiyon aur wadiyon ki Khoobsurti se dil jeet leta hai. Muzaffarabad se le kar Neelum Valley tak har manzar insaan ko apni taraf kheench leta hai. Barf se dhaki chotiyaan, behtay hue jheelon aur chashmon ka paani, aur sabz ghaatiyan is jagah ko asal mein “jannat ka tukda” banati hain. Yahan ke logon ki meheman-nawazi aur mithaas bhari boli is khoobsurat zameen ko aur bhi rangeen bana deti hai. Azad Kashmir sirf ek jagah nahi , balki ek ehsaas hai jo insaan ke dil me sukoon aur khushi bhar deta hai.

Top 5 Places To Visit In Kashmir

  1. Neelam Valey

    • Jisay “Wadi-e-Neelum” bhi kehte hain.
    • Barfili chotiyaan, nadi Neelum, lush green mountains aur waterfalls ki wajah se sabse zyada tourist attract karti hai.

  1. Rawalakot (Pearl Valley)

    • Isay “Shahar-e-Sadaf” ya “Pearl Valley” kaha jata hai.
    • Garmiyon me thandi hawa aur sardiyon me barf, dono ka anokha lutf.

  1. Banjosa Lake

    • Ek man-made lake jo pine trees aur mountains ke beech bas ek painting jaisi lagti hai.
    • Boating aur picnic ke liye bohot mashhoor.

  1. Ratti Gali Lake

    • 12,000 feet ki height par barf se ghiri ek crystal clear alpine lake.
    • Sirf trekking ke zariye pohoncha ja sakta hai, adventure lovers ke liye best PLACE.

  1. Muzaffarabad

    • Azad Jammu & KahmirAzad Jammu & Kahmir ka capital city.
    • Red Fort,Pir Chinasi aur Jhelum & Neelum rivers ke sangam ka manzar bohot khoobsurat hai.

Things To Do In Kashmir

  • Rogan Josh - Kashmiri cuisine ka sabse mashhoor mutton curry, lal mirch aur khas masalon ke sath.
  • Haak Saag - Traditional Kashmiri green leafy dish, rice ke sath bohot pasand ki jati hai.
  • Nature & Adventure - Hiking se aap khoobsurat valleys, lakes aur mountains explore kar sakte ho aur ek adventure ka lutf uthate ho.
  • Stress relief - nature ke andar time guzarne se mind fresh hota hai.
  • Photography & Views
